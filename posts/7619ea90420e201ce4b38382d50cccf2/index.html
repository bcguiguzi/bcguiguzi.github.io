lambdaQuery用法

 LambdaQueryWrapper<RiskControlDetails> wrapper = new LambdaQueryWrapper<>();
        wrapper.eq(RiskControlDetails::getCustomerCode, customerCode);
        RiskControlDetails riskControlDetail = riskControlDetailsMapper.selectOne(wrapper);

.eq() 相等=
.ne()不相等!=
.ge() 大于等于 >=
.le() 小于等于 <=
.gt() 大于>
.lt() 小于<
