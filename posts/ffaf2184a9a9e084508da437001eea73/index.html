第一段 寒暄+基本信息介绍

1、打招呼
Good morning/afternoon,dear teachers/professors！
早上/下午好，尊敬的老师们/教授们！

2、表达感谢（二进一）
① l am glad to be here for this interview.
② Thank you for giving me the opportunity for this interview.
感谢您给我这次面试的机会。

3、基本信息1
My name is 姓名,年龄years old, and l come from家乡.
我叫xx，xx岁，来自xx。

第二段 本科经历+工作情况+考研原因

1、本科基本信息
① l graduated from 本科学校 University in 毕业时间 with a major in 专业名称.
② l had finished my undergraduate education in 本科学校 in 毕业时间 with a major in 专业名称.
我于x年毕业于xx学校xx专业。

2-3、本科学习情况
① 成绩优异
During my college years, I got excellent/satisfactory grades in most of my specialized courses and graduated with a GPA of__. (I had won the the National Scholarship/scholarships … for __ time(s)) , (and I also passed CET-4 and CET-6).
在大学期间，我的大部分专业课都取得了优异的/令人满意的成绩，我以平均学分绩点XXX的成绩毕业，我（获得过x次国家奖学金/奖学金），(并且通过了大学英语四级和六级考试）。
② 成绩一般
During my college years, I took study seriously and grasped the essential knowledge of my major.At the same time, I had broad interests and actively participated in various school events.And I also obtained a certificate of _.
在大学期间，我认真学习，掌握了本专业的基本知识。并且兴趣广泛，积极参与各种学校活动。除此以外，我还获得了XXX证书。

4-5、工作情况
After graduation, I worked as a Legal personnel/teacher/salesman/… in 地方… (and my job duties mainly involves __).
毕业后，我成为了一名软件工程师/教师/销售员/ in某个地方……，我的工作内容主要是XXX。
① 工作与考研专业相关：
My professional knowledge and skills have been further consolidated during this period.
这段工作经历进一步巩固了我的专业知识和技能。
② 工作与考研专业无关：
My cooperation and communication skills have been further improved during this period.
这段工作经历极大地锻炼了我的团队合作能力和交流能力。

6-7、考研原因
① 工作和专业相关
As for why I chose to apply for a master’s degree, there are two reasons:Firstly, after entering the workplace, I realized there is still a lot of room for improvement in my professional knowledge and skills.Secondly,getting a master’s degree can make me more competitive in socicty.

至于我为什么选择攻读研究生，有以下两个原因：一是工作以后，我意识到我的专业知识和技能仍然还有很多提升空间，其次，读研也有利于提高我未来的社会竞争力。

② 工作和专业无关
l chose to apply for a master’s degree because my current job gradually drifted far away from my career plan, and I found my interest lies in XXX after working for _ years, so I want to improve myself and choose a new path towards my desirable career.
我选择考研是因为，目前岗位的工作岗位逐渐不符合我的职业规划，工作了X年后，我发现我对XXX更感兴趣，所以我想通过考研来提升自己，选择新的职业道路。

8-9、备考的努力
l know it is not easy to balance the exam-preparation and my work, but l handled it well by setting a reasonable timetable, and my firm belief in attending graduate school make me stick to the end.
我知道平衡工作和备考并不容易，但通过制定合理的时间表，我处理得还不错。是我对读研的坚定信念让我坚持到了最后。

第三段 性格特点

1、性格
Secondly, as a(n) determined/aggressive/self-disciplined/creative/innovative/motivated/… person, I always strive to achieve higher goals.
其次，作为一个有决心的/有进取心的/自律的/有创造力的/有创新精神的/有上进心的/…的人，我正在努力实现更高的目标。

2、和读研的关系
This kind of personality prompts me to pursue further study on my major.
这种性格促使我继续追求我专业上的学习。

第四段 未来规划&读研展望

1、引入
Next, I want to talk about my future plans.
接下来，我想谈谈研究生期间的规划。

2、夸学校
目标院校 has mighty teaching staff for my dream major and enjoys a high reputation.
贵校在我梦想的专业领域有着雄厚的师资，享有盛誉。

3、表决心
l will take every course in graduate school seriously, and read vast relevant literature to form a more systematic view of 报考专业 and especially of 研究方向. Besides that, I will concentrate on the study on my professional field and work hard to improve my research ability.
我会认真对待研究生每一门课程，并阅读大量相关文献，希望对报考专业特别是研究方向能有一个更系统的认识。除此之外，我将专注于我的专业领域的学习，努力提高我的研究能力。

第五段 结束语

1-2
That’s all about me.If I am lucky enough to be admitted, I will devote myself to the study and research in the next two/three years.
我的自我介绍完毕。如果我有幸被录取，我将在接下来的两年/三年里全身心投入到学习和研究中。
