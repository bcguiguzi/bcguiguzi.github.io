Apache seatunnel集群部署

跳转到安装目录

cd /opt/soft/seatunnel

1.设置环境变量

export SEATUNNEL_HOME=/opt/soft/seatunnel

export PATH=$PATH:$SEATUNNEL_HOME/bin

  1. 启动服务端

./bin/seatunnel-cluster.sh -d

  1. 启动客户端

./bin/seatunnel.sh --config ./config/kafka2gbase_udf.conf

这样就启动成功啦
