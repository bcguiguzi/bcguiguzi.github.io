Docker Desktop解决Creating “rootNode” subnodes问题

Windows/Mac启动Docker Desktop出现

Creating “rootNode” subnodes: constructing “BackendServices” in “rootNode”: doing migrations: reading actual settings: invalid character ‘/’ looking for beginning of value

 

解决方法:

Windows用户删除C:\Users\<UserName>\AppData\Roaming\Docker

Mac用户删除~/Library/Application Support/Docker

如果删除已上文件夹还不能启动

请尝试删除C:\Users\<UserName>\.docker\features.json
